PMSS Nest

Welcome to PMSS Nest, your one-stop destination for all official PMSS (Port Moody Secondary School) merch! Located conveniently within the heart of PMSS in the cafeteria, the Nest stands as the official store for all your school-related needs. Whether you're a student, alumni, or supporter of the PMSS Blues, we've got you covered with a wide range of clothing, stationery, and more, all proudly adorned with the iconic phoenix symbol that represents our school spirit and resilience. We're open during lunch hours on Wednesdays and Fridays, inviting you to drop by and browse through our latest collections. From trendy apparel to essential school supplies, our store caters to all ages and tastes, ensuring that every member of the PMSS community can find something that speaks to them.
